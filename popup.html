How to update your password in Chrome:

  1. Open Chrome settings by clicking the three vertical dots in the top right corner and selecting 'Settings'.
  2. Scroll down and click on 'Passwords' under 'Autofill'.
  3. Find the website in the list and click on the three vertical dots next to it.
  4. Add the password into your Password manager.
